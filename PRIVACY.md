# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 2025 <br>
**Extension:** Qura - Distraction Blocker

---

## Overview

Qura is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your browsing habits and productivity data are yours alone.

---

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
**Nothing.** Qura does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

### What We Store Locally
Qura stores the following data **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Sessions | Your created focus sessions and whitelisted websites |
| Tasks | Tasks you create within each session |
| Statistics | Focus time, completed tasks, and session counts |
| Settings | Theme preferences, blocked message, and toggles |

This data **never leaves your device**.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not:
- ❌ Send data to external servers
- ❌ Use analytics or tracking services
- ❌ Share data with third parties
- ❌ Collect browsing history
- ❌ Store any personally identifiable information

---

## Permissions

Qura requires the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `storage` | Save your sessions, tasks, and settings locally |
| `tabs` | Redirect blocked sites to the blocked page |
| `alarms` | Track elapsed time during focus sessions |
| `host_permissions` (all URLs) | Block distracting websites and inject the task sidebar |

---

## Data Control

You have full control over your data:

- **Export** — Download all your data as a JSON file (Settings → Export Data)
- **Import** — Restore from a previous export (Settings → Import Data)
- **Delete** — Clear all data instantly (Settings → Reset All Data)
- **Uninstall** — Removing the extension deletes all stored data

---

## Third-Party Services

Qura does not integrate with any third-party services. The only external link is the optional Ko-fi donation button, which opens in a new tab and does not transmit any data.
